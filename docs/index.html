iteration 1 — web prototype

the css div era

buddy was just a div with a face. no canvas. no electron. just vibes and dreams. he couldn't even walk he just sat there judging us.

iteration 2 — electron app

he learned to walk

split into two windows. buddy got his own transparent always-on-top window. drew him pixel by pixel on canvas. he took his first steps and we literally cheered.

iteration 3 — the auto-cancel era

we got too ambitious ngl

added puppeteer-core to auto-cancel. encrypted credentials. screenshot proof. then we realized CAPTCHAs exist and the whole thing was a war crime waiting to happen.

iteration 4 — reality check

googled competitors. cried.

rocket money. donotpay. trim. bobby. all doing the same thing. realized the buddy IS the product not the automation. pivoted harder than a tiktok trend.

iteration 5 — the $0.69/mo mistake

we tried to charge money lol

added stripe. added a server. added webhooks. added device IDs. added a paywall. then realized we're charging people money to save money and that's just a pyramid scheme with extra steps.

iteration 6 — current. finally. we think.

nuked everything. kept the buddy.

removed stripe. removed the server. removed accounts. removed the paywall. 5 files. no dependencies except electron. free for 2 months then optional UPI donation. this is the version. probably. unless we spiral again.
